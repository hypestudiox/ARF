About Asian Racing Federation

The Asian Racing Federation (ARF) is a regional federation comprising 29 national racing authorities and racing-related organisations from across Asia, Oceania, Africa and the Middle East.

Asian Racing Conference

The Asian Racing Conference (ARC) is a major event on the international racing calendar. Attracting and engaging the foremost leaders and greatest minds in racing and sports, along with other influential stakeholders, the ARC aims to shape global and regional agendas to continuously advance the racing industry.

Anti-Illegal Betting

The Council on Anti-Illegal Betting & Related Financial Crime fosters and enhances international cooperation among horse racing operators, regulators, intergovernmental organisations and government agencies in order to better combat the threat of illegal betting and other financial crimes to horse racing integrity in particular, and to sport in general.
